Practical Application and Assessment

This lesson focuses on consolidating knowledge of hardware and software through research and testing.

Activity

With a partner, conduct research on the following topics. Discuss your findings and answer the questions below:

  • Additional examples of hardware components and their functions.
  • Identify software not listed previously and explain its use.

Test Questions

  1. What is hardware? Provide three examples.
  2. Describe two types of software and their purposes.
  3. What new information did you find from your research?

Reminder: Think carefully before answering. You have two chances to complete each test.
